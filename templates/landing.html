Welcome!

PyMNtos (pronounced pie-min-tose) is a Twin Cities group of Python enthusiasts. Some of us use Python every day in our work. Some of us are just hobbyists. Others are just learning. Whoever you are and whatever your skill level is, we would love for you to join us.

We meet at 7:00 PM on the second Thursday of the month at The Nerdery, in Bloomington. There is no membership fee or any other barrier to entry. Just RSVP so that the Nerdery knows how much pizza to get.
